Our Journey, Vision & Team

Empowering the next generation of tech innovators since 2010.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate educators, Maa Digital Computer Education started as a modest classroom in a shared co‑working space. Over the past decade we have grown into a premier institute, offering a comprehensive curriculum that spans from basic computer literacy to advanced software development and AI research. Our alumni have gone on to secure positions at leading tech firms, launch successful startups, and contribute to open‑source communities worldwide.

2010

Institute founded; first batch of 20 students.

2014

Introduced full‑stack web development program.

2018

Launched AI & Machine Learning specialization.

2022

Opened state‑of‑the‑art campus with modern labs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver high‑quality, industry‑relevant education that equips learners with the skills, confidence, and mindset needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Vision

To become the leading catalyst for digital transformation in the region, fostering a community of innovators who shape the future of technology.
